"Ashavan — Forest of Hopes — is a compassionate animal welfare initiative dedicated to healing, and protecting street and abandoned animals while reconnecting humans with kindness and nature.

We believe that when animals feel safe, humanity becomes stronger.

Ashavan is not just an organization — it is a growing movement of compassion, responsibility, and conscious coexistence."

What We Believe

At Ashavan, we believe every life matters — whether it walks, flies, crawls, or speaks without words.

Compassion is not an act — it is a way of living.

Service to animals is service to humanity and nature together.

Ashavan — meaning “Forest of Hopes”

Our Mission

Our mission is to build a kinder connection between animals, people, and nature through community participation and humane education.

Ashavan is growing as a compassion-driven movement, working toward long-term, sustainable solutions and future Happiness Centres where healing and harmony can exist together.
